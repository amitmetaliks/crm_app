TRIAM A+ — Privacy Policy

Amit Metaliks Limited (Amit Alliance). Last updated: June 2026.

TRIAM A+ is an internal field-sales application provided by Amit Metaliks Limited to its own employees (sales representatives and managers). It is not offered to the general public. This policy explains what data the app collects and why.

Information we collect

  • Account & profile — your employee name, designation, company email/phone (from your employer's HR records).
  • Location (GPS) — captured at visit check-in/out and attendance, and periodically while you are on duty, to verify dealer visits, record your route, and compute travel distance.
    When route recording is on: on the Android app, recording starts when you mark attendance check-in and stops at check-out. While it is running, a permanent notification ("TRIAM A+ — on duty") stays visible so you always know your route is being recorded. Recording continues if your screen switches off during working hours. It does not run before check-in, after check-out, or on your off days, and the app does not collect location silently in the background.
  • Photos — selfies for attendance and photos you take during dealer visits, stamped with date/time/location.
  • Work data — visits, orders, expenses, attendance, leave you record.
  • Device info — basic technical data needed to operate the app.

How we use it

Solely for legitimate workforce management: verifying field visits and attendance, route and expense calculation, sales reporting, and payroll/accounting within the company's own ERP system.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising. Data is stored on the company's own/managed servers and is accessible only to authorised company staff.

Retention & your rights

Data is retained per company policy and statutory requirements. To access, correct, or request deletion of your data, contact your HR/admin or the address below.

Security

Access is restricted by login; the app supports a PIN/biometric lock. Connections use HTTPS.
